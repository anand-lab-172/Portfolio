01About & Stack

I'm a data scientist with roots in mechanical engineering — which shows up in how I think about models: as systems with inputs, tolerances, and failure modes, not just accuracy scores.

Over the last five years I've moved from forecasting EV battery health at Renault-Nissan, to predicting satellite network usage at Intelsat, to leading applied AI for Nissan's manufacturing operations at Genpact — building GenAI assistants, forecasting pipelines, and internal tools that cross-functional teams rely on daily.

Comfortable owning a problem end-to-end: from a 500M-row BigQuery table, through model training and MLOps tracking, to a dashboard a plant manager can actually read.
